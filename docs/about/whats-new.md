# What's new

## August 2025

August has been a vibrant month for our Fabric Adoption Framework community, and I'm excited to share some updates that I believe will enhance your experience. We’ve made significant strides in refining our documentation, including the addition of a new License section and a corresponding link in the navigation menu. This change not only clarifies our usage policies but also makes it easier for you to find important information at a glance (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-2fcc4ca958e093e082d40a93ab14ba804b31822d.md). 

In our ongoing effort to streamline your journey through our resources, we’ve also collapsed sidebar categories for greater clarity. This adjustment is aimed at making navigation more intuitive, allowing you to find what you need without feeling overwhelmed (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-07-428a3d2df5e70d8c5e77d1111e8edbf72439302d.md). Additionally, we’ve synchronized new content and strategy documents to ensure you have access to the latest insights and methodologies that can support your adoption journey (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-9993fe2ef400b1868a1874a379e6fe864b68fd0c.md).

I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to all contributors who helped bring these updates to life. Your dedication makes a real difference in how our community engages with the framework. I’m looking forward to seeing how these changes impact your experience as we continue to grow together!
