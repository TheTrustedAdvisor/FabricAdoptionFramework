## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This June, we made some significant changes to our documentation that I believe will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. Most notably, we decided to remove the "What's New" documentation section. While it served its purpose, we felt that consolidating information and streamlining our content would make it easier for you to find what you need without sifting through multiple updates. You can read more about this decision [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

This change matters because it reflects our commitment to providing you with a more cohesive and user-friendly resource. By simplifying our navigation and focusing on the core content, we hope to make your journey through the documentation smoother and more intuitive. I truly believe that this will foster a better understanding of the framework and how to implement it effectively in your projects.

I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to everyone in our community who has contributed to this process. Your feedback and insights are invaluable, and they guide us in making improvements that truly matter to you. I’m excited to see how these updates will positively impact your experience as you engage with the Fabric Adoption Framework.
