# What's new

## August 2025

Hello, dear readers! I’m excited to share some wonderful updates from August that I believe will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. This month, we’ve made significant strides in organizing our content and improving navigation. We’ve collapsed sidebar categories for clarity, making it easier for you to find what you need without feeling overwhelmed (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-07-428a3d2df5e70d8c5e77d1111e8edbf72439302d.md). Additionally, we’ve expanded the visibility of our strategy category, ensuring that the resources you seek are more accessible than ever (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-7d86c741c0df7564954cd2fd203e9fcb7d50b956.md).

We also took a moment to refresh our documentation with new content and a clearer structure. This includes the addition of a License section and a corresponding link in the navigation menu, which not only clarifies our governance but also aligns with best practices for transparency (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-2fcc4ca958e093e082d40a93ab14ba804b31822d.md) (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-5c7af7d42f049ba974347b811dbea587eedf7a00.md). These changes are designed to empower you on your journey, making it easier to navigate our resources and understand the framework.

I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to all the contributors and the community for your ongoing support and collaboration. Your feedback and engagement truly make a difference. I hope these updates enrich your experience and help you in your adoption journey! ## July 2025

Hello everyone! I’m excited to share some wonderful updates from July 2025 that I believe will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. This month, we’ve made significant strides by establishing initial content for the framework, which aims to provide a clearer understanding of how to effectively adopt and integrate our solutions. You can dive into the details of this foundational content [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-07-20-b6ea8bd71edcd6fcab2d774df9ea7b7b415bcbc2.md). 

In addition, we’ve worked on refining our documentation by removing some initial files that were no longer relevant, allowing us to streamline the information you see. This not only declutters the navigation but also ensures that you can easily find what you need without sifting through outdated materials. If you’re curious about what we’ve removed, you can read more about it [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-07-20-3948fa7bc9ab671af8690e6527e831adebbec1dc.md).

I truly believe these changes will create a more cohesive and user-friendly experience for everyone engaging with our framework. A heartfelt thank you to all contributors and the community for your ongoing support and dedication in making this happen. Together, we’re building something special! ## June 2025

Hello everyone! I’m excited to share some recent updates to our Fabric Adoption Framework documentation that I think you’ll find quite impactful. This month, we made a significant change by removing the "What's New" documentation section. While it may seem like a small adjustment, it reflects our commitment to streamlining our content and ensuring that you can find the information you need more easily. You can read more about this change [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

Why does this matter? By consolidating our documentation, we aim to enhance your experience as a reader. We want to make sure that every piece of information is accessible and relevant to you without unnecessary clutter. This change is part of our ongoing effort to refine our content and improve navigation, making it easier for you to engage with the resources we provide. 

I want to take a moment to thank our contributors and the community for their feedback and support, which have been invaluable in guiding these decisions. We believe that these updates will foster a more intuitive experience as you explore the Fabric Adoption Framework. I’m looking forward to seeing how these changes positively impact your journey with us!
