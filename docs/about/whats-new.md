# What's new

## August 2025

Hello, dear readers! I’m excited to share some wonderful updates from August that I believe will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. This month, we’ve made significant strides in organizing our documentation and improving accessibility. For instance, we’ve collapsed sidebar categories for clarity, making it easier for you to navigate through the content and find exactly what you need without feeling overwhelmed (Read more ›).

We also added a License section to our documentation, along with a direct link in the navigation menu. This change is essential as it clarifies the usage rights and ensures you have all the necessary information at your fingertips (Read more ›). Additionally, we synchronized our published strategy methodology documents, which means you can now access the most up-to-date strategies for Fabric adoption in one place (Read more ›). 

These updates are all about making your journey smoother and more intuitive as you explore the framework. I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to everyone who contributed to these changes. Your efforts truly make a difference! I hope these enhancements help you feel more empowered in your adoption journey. ## June 2025

Hello dear readers! This June, we made a significant change to our documentation structure by removing the "What's New" section. While it served us well for keeping you updated, we felt it was time to streamline our content and make room for more relevant information that directly supports your journey with the Fabric Adoption Framework.
