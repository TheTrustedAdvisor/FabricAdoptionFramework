# What's new

## August 2025

Hello, dear readers! August has been a vibrant month for the Fabric Adoption Framework, and I’m excited to share some wonderful updates that enhance your experience with our documentation. We’ve made significant strides in refining our content and structure, ensuring that you can navigate our resources with greater ease and clarity. One of the key changes is the addition of a License section in our documentation, which not only clarifies usage rights but also fosters transparency (Read more ›). Additionally, we’ve revamped the navigation menu to include a direct link to this new section, making it more accessible for you (Read more ›).

We’ve also taken steps to improve the visibility of our strategy category within the documentation. By expanding this area, we aim to provide you with more straightforward access to the strategies that can guide your Fabric adoption journey (Read more ›). I believe these changes matter because they empower you to find the information you need more quickly, helping you to make informed decisions as you navigate your adoption process. 

Lastly, I want to express my heartfelt gratitude to our contributors and the community for their ongoing support and collaboration. Your feedback and engagement are what drive these improvements, and I can’t wait to see how these updates will positively impact your experience. Together, we’re building a more user-friendly and informative resource for everyone involved in Fabric adoption.
