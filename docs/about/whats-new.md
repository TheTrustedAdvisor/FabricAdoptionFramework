Hello, dear readers! August has been a busy month for us at the Fabric Adoption Framework, and I'm excited to share some meaningful updates that enhance your experience with our documentation. This not only clarifies the usage rights but also reflects our commitment to transparency and openness in our community (Read more ›).

I want to extend a heartfelt thank you to everyone who contributed to these enhancements. Your feedback and support play a vital role in shaping our documentation, and I hope these updates make your experience more enjoyable and productive. Together, we're building a community that thrives on collaboration and shared knowledge. Let’s keep moving forward! ## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This month, we've made a significant change to our documentation structure that I want to share with you. We've removed the "What's New" section from our website. This decision was made to streamline our content and make it easier for you to find the information you need without the clutter of updates that may not always be relevant to your journey. You can read more about this change [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

Why does this matter? By simplifying our navigation and focusing on the most pertinent content, we hope to enhance your experience as you explore the Fabric Adoption Framework. It’s all about ensuring that you can easily access the resources that will help you on your path, without sifting through sections that no longer serve our community's needs. I genuinely believe that this change will create a more seamless experience for all of you.

I want to take a moment to thank everyone who contributed to this decision and the ongoing improvements to our documentation. Your feedback and support are invaluable, and I’m excited to see how these changes will positively impact your experience with the framework. Together, we’re building something truly special.
