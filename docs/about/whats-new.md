# What's new

## August 2025

Hello, dear readers! I’m excited to share some wonderful updates we’ve made to the Fabric Adoption Framework this August. We’ve been busy refining our documentation to enhance your experience. One of the most significant changes is the addition of a new License section, which not only clarifies how you can use our materials but also now has its own link in the navigation menu for easy access (Read more ›). This should make it simpler for you to find important information about usage rights.

In our continuous effort to improve clarity, we’ve also collapsed sidebar categories, making navigation more straightforward and user-friendly (Read more ›). This change allows you to focus on what matters most without feeling overwhelmed by options. Additionally, we’ve synchronized our published strategy methodology documents and expanded the visibility of our strategy categories in the documentation (Read more ›). These updates aim to provide you with a clearer path to understanding and implementing our strategies effectively.

I want to extend a heartfelt thank you to our contributors and the community for your ongoing support and feedback. Your insights drive these improvements, and we hope these changes make your journey with the Fabric Adoption Framework even more enjoyable and productive. Together, we’re creating a resource that truly meets your needs!
