I truly appreciate the contributions from our community that have made these updates possible, and I’m hopeful that these changes will foster a more engaging and user-friendly experience for everyone.

## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This June, we made some significant changes to our documentation structure that I believe will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. Most notably, we've decided to remove the "What's New" section from our documentation. This decision stems from our desire to streamline the content and make navigation more intuitive for you. By focusing on the most relevant and impactful information, we hope to create a more cohesive reading experience. You can read more about this change [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

I truly appreciate the feedback from our community that led us to this decision. It matters because we want to ensure that you can easily find the information you need without sifting through sections that may no longer serve you. By refining our documentation, we expect to foster a more engaging and user-friendly environment, allowing you to focus on what truly matters in your journey with us.

Thank you for your continued support and contributions, which make our documentation better every day. Together, we're creating a resource that reflects your needs and aspirations. If you're curious about the details of this update and others, feel free to explore the full list of changes [here](https://github.com/TheTrustedAdvisor/FabricAdoptionFramework/commits/main?since=2025-06-01&until=2025-06-30).
