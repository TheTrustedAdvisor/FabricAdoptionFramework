## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This month has brought some significant changes to our Fabric Adoption Framework documentation that I’m excited to share with you. We’ve made the decision to remove the “What’s New” documentation section from our site. This change is aimed at streamlining our content and making it easier for you to find the information you need without sifting through updates that may not be relevant to your journey. You can read more about this decision and its implications [here](https://github.com/TheTrustedAdvisor/FabricAdoptionFramework/commits/main?since=2025-06-01&until=2025-06-30).

I truly believe that this shift will enhance your experience as you navigate our resources. It’s all about making your journey smoother and more enjoyable, and I’m grateful for the community’s feedback that helped guide this decision.

Thank you for being such an integral part of our journey. Your insights and engagement drive us to continuously improve, and I’m looking forward to seeing how these changes positively impact your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. Let’s keep moving forward together! (Read more ›)
