Hello dear readers! I'm excited to share some wonderful updates from August that are designed to enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. In addition to these changes, we’ve streamlined our navigation by adding a License section, which is now easily accessible from the menu (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-5c7af7d42f049ba974347b811dbea587eedf7a00.md). These updates are all about making your journey smoother and more enjoyable. A heartfelt thank you to everyone who contributed to these improvements; your efforts truly make a difference! I hope you find these changes helpful as you explore the Fabric Adoption Framework. (531 commits)

## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This June, we made some significant changes to our documentation that I’m excited to share with you. We’ve decided to remove the "What's New" documentation section. This decision came after thoughtful consideration of how best to present our updates and ensure you can easily find the information that truly matters to you. By streamlining our content, we hope to create a more focused experience that highlights the most relevant resources for your journey with the Fabric Adoption Framework. You can read more about this change [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).
