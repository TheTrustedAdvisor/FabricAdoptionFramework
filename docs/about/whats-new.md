## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This month, we made a significant change to our documentation structure by removing the "What's New" section. While it was a familiar feature, we realized that it wasn't serving our community as effectively as we'd hoped. By streamlining our content, we aim to create a more cohesive experience that allows you to focus on the most relevant information without the clutter. If you're curious about the specific changes made, you can read more about it [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

This decision was driven by your feedback and our commitment to enhancing your experience on our site. We understand that navigating documentation can sometimes feel overwhelming, and we believe that simplifying our structure will make it easier for you to find what you need. We hope this change will encourage you to explore more of our resources without feeling bogged down by unnecessary sections. A heartfelt thank you to everyone who contributed to this discussion and shared their insights!

As we move forward, I truly believe this shift will foster a more engaging and user-friendly environment for all of you. I’m excited for what’s to come and hope you are too! Together, we can continue to build a thriving community around the Fabric Adoption Framework.
