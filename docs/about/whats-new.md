While it has served as a way to keep you updated, we felt it was time to streamline our content and make navigation a bit more intuitive. This change will help you focus more on the core materials without the distraction of a separate updates section (Read more › /about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to everyone who contributed to this decision and the changes that followed. As always, we’re here to support you on your path to adoption and success.
