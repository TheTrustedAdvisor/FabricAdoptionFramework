## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This June, we made some significant changes to our documentation that I believe will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. One of the most notable updates is the removal of the "What's New" documentation section. We realized that consolidating our updates into a more streamlined format would make it easier for you to find the information that truly matters. This change is part of our ongoing effort to simplify navigation and improve the overall structure of our content (Read more › /about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

Your feedback has been instrumental in guiding these decisions, and I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to everyone who contributed their thoughts and suggestions (Read more › /about/changes/2025-06-03-3a35144aa92198574f983f6a3054dd2b5db3ab9c.md).
