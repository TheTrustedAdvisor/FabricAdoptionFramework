## June 2025

Hello dear readers! I’m excited to share some updates from June that reflect our commitment to making the Fabric Adoption Framework more user-friendly and intuitive for you. This month, we made a significant change by removing the "What's New" documentation section. While it may seem like a small shift, this decision was made to streamline our content and help you find the information you need without any distractions. You can read more about this change [here](https://about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

I truly appreciate the feedback from our community that helped guide this decision.

As we move forward, I hope these adjustments will make your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework even more enjoyable and productive.
