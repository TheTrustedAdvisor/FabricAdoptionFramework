## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This June, we've made some significant changes to our documentation that I believe will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. Most notably, we've decided to remove the "What's New" section from our documentation. While it served a purpose, we felt that consolidating our updates into more streamlined content would make it easier for you to find the information you need without sifting through multiple sections. You can read more about this decision [here](https://about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

This change matters because it reflects our commitment to improving the overall navigation and structure of our documentation. By eliminating redundancy, we hope to create a more cohesive experience that allows you to focus on the content that truly matters to your journey with our framework. I genuinely appreciate the feedback from our community that guided us in making this decision, and I’m excited to see how it enhances your interactions with our resources.

As we move forward, I anticipate that this streamlined approach will not only save you time but also make it easier for you to stay updated with the latest developments in a more integrated way. Thank you for being an essential part of our community and for your continued support as we evolve our documentation to better serve you. If you’d like to explore all the changes made this month, feel free to check out the [19 commits](https://github.com/TheTrustedAdvisor/FabricAdoptionFramework/commits/main?since=2025-06-01&until=2025-06-30).
