Hello everyone! I’m excited to share some wonderful updates from August that enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. You’ll notice that we’ve added a new License section to the documentation, which is now easily accessible through the navigation menu. This change not only clarifies our licensing terms but also ensures you have all the necessary information at your fingertips (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-2fcc4ca958e093e082d40a93ab14ba804b31822d.md).

This means you can now find the information you need more intuitively, making your journey through the framework smoother and more engaging (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-f359f7af9238c768ef15da3bd9b4a18af45e0d78.md). I truly believe these updates will empower you to navigate our resources with greater ease and confidence.

Lastly, we’ve made some adjustments to the sidebar to collapse categories for clarity, which should help in reducing clutter and enhancing your reading experience. A heartfelt thank you to everyone who contributed to these changes; your input is invaluable to us (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-07-428a3d2df5e70d8c5e77d1111e8edbf72439302d.md). Together, we’re making the Fabric Adoption Framework a more welcoming and user-friendly space for all. (531 commits)
