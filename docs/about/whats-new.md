## August 2025

Hello, dear readers! I’m excited to share some heartwarming updates from the Fabric Adoption Framework this August. We’ve made several thoughtful changes to enhance your experience navigating our documentation. For starters, we’ve expanded the visibility of our strategy category, making it easier for you to find the resources you need to support your Fabric adoption journey (Read more ›). Additionally, we’ve streamlined our sidebar by collapsing categories for clarity, ensuring that you can focus on what matters most without feeling overwhelmed (Read more ›).

We’ve also added a new License section to our documentation, along with a link in the navigation menu. This is a crucial step in promoting transparency and helping you understand the terms under which our resources are shared (Read more ›). These changes reflect our commitment to creating a user-friendly environment that encourages exploration and learning, and I truly believe they will make a positive impact on how you engage with our content.

I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to everyone who contributed to these updates. Your dedication helps us grow and improve continuously. Together, we’re building a supportive community around the Fabric Adoption Framework, and I can’t wait to see how these enhancements will help you on your path.
