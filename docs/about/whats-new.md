Hello, dear readers! I’m excited to share some wonderful updates from August that I believe will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. You can find out more about these additions [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-08-08-2fcc4ca958e093e082d40a93ab14ba804b31822d.md) and [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-08-08-5c7af7d42f049ba974347b811dbea587eedf7a00.md).

I want to extend a heartfelt thank you to all our contributors and the community for your ongoing support and input. Your engagement helps us continually improve and adapt our framework to better serve you. I’m looking forward to seeing how these changes impact your journey with us! ## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This June, we made some significant changes to our documentation that I believe will enhance your experience navigating the Fabric Adoption Framework. We decided to remove the "What's New" documentation section, which was previously intended to keep you updated on recent changes. While it served its purpose, we felt it was time to streamline our content and make it easier for you to find the information you truly need without unnecessary clutter. You can read more about this decision [here](https://www.fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

This change matters because it reflects our commitment to providing you with a more user-friendly experience. By removing redundant sections, we aim to create a cleaner and more intuitive navigation path for you. Our goal is to ensure that you can easily access the information that will help you adopt and utilize our framework effectively. I genuinely believe that these adjustments will lead to a more satisfying and productive experience for everyone involved.

I want to take a moment to thank our contributors and the community for their ongoing support and feedback. Your insights are invaluable, and they guide our decisions as we strive to improve our documentation. I’m excited about the positive impact these changes will have, and I look forward to hearing your thoughts!
