Hello, dear readers! August has been a bustling month for the Fabric Adoption Framework, and I’m excited to share some meaningful updates that will enhance your experience with our documentation. We’ve made significant strides in organizing our content, including collapsing sidebar categories for improved clarity. This change aims to streamline your navigation, making it easier for you to find what you need without feeling overwhelmed (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-07-428a3d2df5e70d8c5e77d1111e8edbf72439302d.md).

In addition to the structural updates, we’ve also added a License section to our documentation, along with a link in the navigation menu. This addition is important as it provides transparency and clarity regarding the use of our materials (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-2fcc4ca958e093e082d40a93ab14ba804b31822d.md) (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-5c7af7d42f049ba974347b811dbea587eedf7a00.md). We believe these changes will foster a stronger sense of trust and accessibility for everyone engaging with our framework.

Lastly, we’ve synchronized and expanded our strategy documentation, ensuring that you have the most up-to-date resources at your fingertips. This effort reflects our commitment to supporting your journey in adopting Fabric effectively (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-9993fe2ef400b1868a1874a379e6fe864b68fd0c.md). Thank you to all our contributors and the community for your ongoing support and collaboration. Together, we’re making great strides! (531 commits)
