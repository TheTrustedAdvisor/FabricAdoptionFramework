Hello, dear readers! I’m excited to share some wonderful updates from August that I believe will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. For instance, we’ve added a new License section to our documentation, which is now accessible directly from the navigation menu. This change not only clarifies our usage guidelines but also reinforces our commitment to transparency (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-2fcc4ca958e093e082d40a93ab14ba804b31822d.md).

In addition to that, we’ve been busy synchronizing our content to reflect the latest strategies for Fabric adoption. This means you’ll find updated methodology documents that are now more visible in the strategy category, making it simpler for you to navigate through our resources (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-f359f7af9238c768ef15da3bd9b4a18af45e0d78.md). These updates are all about making your journey with us smoother and more intuitive. A heartfelt thank you to everyone who contributed to these changes; your efforts truly make a difference! I hope you enjoy exploring the refreshed content and find it even more helpful as you navigate the Fabric Adoption Framework. (531 commits)

## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This month, we made a significant change to our documentation structure that I believe will enhance your experience. We decided to remove the "What's New" documentation section. This decision was made after careful consideration of how we can streamline our content and make it easier for you to find the information you need without sifting through updates that may not be relevant to your journey. You can read more about this change [here](https://github.com/TheTrustedAdvisor/FabricAdoptionFramework/commits/main?since=2025-06-01&until=2025-06-30).

Why does this matter? By simplifying our navigation and focusing on the most pertinent information, we hope to create a more intuitive experience for you. We want you to spend less time searching and more time engaging with the content that truly supports your adoption of the Fabric framework. Our goal is to make your journey smoother and more enjoyable, and we believe this change is a step in that direction.

I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to our contributors and community for their ongoing feedback and support. Your insights are invaluable, and they help us shape a documentation experience that truly serves you. I’m excited about the positive impact these changes will bring, and I can’t wait for you to explore the updated site! (Read more ›)
