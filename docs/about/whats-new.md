## August 2025

August has been a transformative month for the Fabric Adoption Framework, and I'm excited to share the updates that we've made to enhance your experience. One of the most significant changes is the addition of a dedicated License section in our documentation, along with a link to it in the navigation menu. This not only clarifies our usage policies but also makes it easier for you to find essential information about how to engage with our resources (Read more ›).

We’ve also streamlined our content structure by collapsing sidebar categories for improved clarity. This means you can now navigate through our documentation more intuitively, allowing you to focus on what matters most without feeling overwhelmed (Read more ›). Additionally, we've synchronized and expanded our strategy category visibility, ensuring that you have access to the most relevant methodologies for your Fabric adoption journey (Read more ›). 

I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to our contributors and community for their ongoing support and input, which have been invaluable in shaping these updates. Together, we're making it easier for everyone to adopt and benefit from the Fabric framework. I can’t wait for you to explore these changes!
