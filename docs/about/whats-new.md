Hello, dear readers! I’m excited to share some wonderful updates from August that will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. This month, we’ve made significant strides in improving our documentation structure and clarity. One of the most notable changes is the addition of a License section to our documentation, which you can now easily find in the navigation menu. This not only helps clarify usage rights but also fosters a more transparent relationship with our community (Read more ›).

I believe these changes will make your journey through our documentation smoother and more enjoyable.

Lastly, I want to extend a heartfelt thank you to all the contributors and community members who have helped us refine our content and structure. Your feedback and support are invaluable in making our documentation a welcoming space for everyone. I’m looking forward to seeing how these updates positively impact your experience! ## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This June, we’ve made some significant changes to our documentation that I’m excited to share with you. Most notably, we’ve decided to remove the "What's New" documentation section. This was a tough decision, but we believe it will streamline your experience and make it easier for you to find the information you need without sifting through updates that may not be relevant to your journey. You can read more about this change [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

Why does this matter? By simplifying our content structure, we hope to enhance your navigation experience. We want you to spend less time searching and more time engaging with the resources that truly support your adoption of the Fabric framework. Our goal is to create a more intuitive experience that reflects your needs and feedback. A heartfelt thank you to everyone in our community who contributed to this decision; your insights are invaluable!

Looking ahead, we expect these changes to foster a more cohesive and user-friendly environment. As we continue to evolve, your input will remain crucial in shaping our documentation. I’m eager to see how these updates resonate with you, and I appreciate your ongoing support as we strive to make your experience even better. Together, we’re building something great!
