## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This month, we made a significant shift in how we present our updates. We decided to remove the "What's New" documentation section entirely. While this might seem like a big change, it’s all about streamlining our content and making it easier for you to find the information you need without unnecessary clutter. You can read more about this decision in detail [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

By simplifying our structure, we aim to enhance your experience as you navigate through our resources. We believe that this change will allow you to focus on the core content that truly matters to you, without sifting through a separate section for updates. It’s all about creating a more cohesive and user-friendly environment for everyone involved in the Fabric Adoption Framework journey. I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to our contributors and community members for their valuable feedback, which guided us in making this decision.

As we move forward, I’m excited to see how these adjustments will positively impact your experience.
