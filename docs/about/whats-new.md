# What's new

## August 2025

Hello everyone! I’m excited to share some wonderful updates from August that I believe will enhance your experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. This month, we’ve made significant strides in organizing our content and improving navigation. For instance, we've collapsed sidebar categories for clarity, making it easier for you to find what you need without feeling overwhelmed (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-07-428a3d2df5e70d8c5e77d1111e8edbf72439302d.md). Additionally, we’ve added a License section to our documentation, which is now easily accessible via the navigation menu (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-5c7af7d42f049ba974347b811dbea587eedf7a00.md). 

These changes matter because they reflect our commitment to creating a user-friendly environment where you can navigate our resources effortlessly. By streamlining the layout and providing clear access to important information, we hope to empower you to engage with the framework more confidently and effectively. 

I also want to extend my heartfelt thanks to our contributors and the community for their ongoing support and feedback, which have been invaluable in shaping these updates. Together, we’re building a more accessible and welcoming space for everyone interested in fabric adoption strategies. I can’t wait to see how these improvements impact your journey with us! ## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This month, we made a significant change to our documentation structure by removing the "What's New" section. This decision was made after careful consideration and feedback from our community, as we aim to streamline the information you access and enhance your overall experience on our site. By focusing on a more integrated approach to updates, we hope to make it easier for you to find the information that truly matters to you without sifting through separate sections. You can read more about this change [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

It's all about ensuring that you can navigate our documentation effortlessly and find what you need when you need it. I genuinely appreciate the contributions and insights from our community that guided us in this direction.

As we move forward, I anticipate that this shift will not only enhance your experience but also foster a more cohesive understanding of our evolving framework. Thank you for being part of this journey with us; your feedback is invaluable.
