I hope these enhancements help you on your journey with the Fabric Adoption Framework! (531 commits)

## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This month, we made a significant change to our documentation structure by removing the "What's New" section. I know this might come as a surprise, but we believe that consolidating our updates into a more streamlined format will enhance your experience. By focusing on integrating these updates directly into relevant sections of our documentation, you can find the information you need without having to navigate through a separate area. This change is all about making your journey through our resources smoother and more intuitive. If you're interested in the details of this update, you can read more about it [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

The decision to remove the "What's New" section stems from our commitment to providing a more cohesive and user-friendly experience. We want to ensure that you can easily access the most relevant information without sifting through multiple areas. This change is expected to foster a more integrated approach to how we share updates and enhancements, ultimately making it easier for you to stay informed about the latest developments in our framework. 

I want to take a moment to thank everyone in our community for their continued support and feedback, which have been invaluable in guiding these changes. Your input helps us create a better experience for all users, and I’m excited to see how this new structure will benefit you. As always, we’re here to support your journey, and I encourage you to explore the updated documentation!
