# What's new

## August 2025

Hello, dear readers! August has been a bustling month for the Fabric Adoption Framework, and I’m excited to share some wonderful updates that enhance your experience with our documentation. We’ve made significant strides in organizing our content to make it more accessible and user-friendly. Notably, we’ve collapsed sidebar categories for clarity, which should help you navigate through the information more smoothly (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-07-428a3d2df5e70d8c5e77d1111e8edbf72439302d.md). 

In addition to the structural changes, we’ve also added a License section to our documentation, along with a link in the navigation menu. This addition is important because it provides transparency and clarity about how our content can be used (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-2fcc4ca958e093e082d40a93ab14ba804b31822d.md). We believe these updates will foster a better understanding of our framework and encourage more community engagement.

Lastly, we’ve synchronized our published strategies for Fabric adoption and expanded the visibility of our strategy category within the documentation. This means you’ll have easier access to the resources you need to successfully implement Fabric in your projects (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-9993fe2ef400b1868a1874a379e6fe864b68fd0c.md). A heartfelt thank you to all the contributors and community members who have made these changes possible. Your support is invaluable! (531 commits)
