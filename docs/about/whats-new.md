## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This June, we made some significant changes to our documentation structure that I believe will enhance your experience. Most notably, we decided to remove the "What's New" section from our site. This was a tough choice, but we felt it was time to streamline our content and make it easier for you to find the information you need without the clutter of redundant updates. If you want to dive deeper into the details of this change, you can read more about it [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

Why does this matter? By simplifying our navigation and focusing on the most relevant content, we hope to create a more intuitive experience for you. We want you to spend less time searching for updates and more time engaging with the material that truly matters to your work. This change is all about enhancing clarity and usability, making your journey through our documentation smoother and more enjoyable.

I want to extend a heartfelt thank you to our contributors and community for your ongoing support and feedback. Your insights are invaluable in shaping our resources, and I’m excited to see how these changes will impact your experience moving forward. Together, we’re building something special!
