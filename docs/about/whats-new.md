## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This month, we made a significant shift in our documentation structure by removing the "What's New" section entirely. While it might seem like a small change, I believe it will help streamline your experience by allowing us to integrate updates more seamlessly throughout the documentation. You can read more about this change [here](https://github.com/TheTrustedAdvisor/FabricAdoptionFramework/commits/main?since=2025-06-01&until=2025-06-30).

Why does this matter? Well, by eliminating the standalone "What's New" section, we aim to enhance the overall flow of information, making it easier for you to find the latest updates directly within the relevant content. This approach reflects our commitment to providing a more cohesive and user-friendly experience, ensuring that you can access the information you need without unnecessary navigation. I genuinely appreciate the feedback from our community that guided us in this direction.

As we move forward, I expect this change to foster a more integrated understanding of our documentation. Thank you for your continued support and engagement; it truly makes a difference! I’m excited to see how this evolves our shared journey in the Fabric Adoption Framework. (Read more ›)
