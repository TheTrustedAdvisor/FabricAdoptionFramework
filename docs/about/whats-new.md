# What's new

## August 2025

Hello, dear readers! August has brought some exciting updates to the Fabric Adoption Framework that I can’t wait to share with you. We’ve made significant strides in enhancing our documentation, including the addition of a new License section and a corresponding link in our navigation menu. This change not only clarifies our usage terms but also makes it easier for you to find essential information at a glance (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-2fcc4ca958e093e082d40a93ab14ba804b31822d.md). 

We’ve also streamlined the user experience by collapsing sidebar categories for better clarity. This means you can navigate through our content more smoothly, allowing you to focus on what truly matters—adopting and implementing our strategies effectively (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-07-428a3d2df5e70d8c5e77d1111e8edbf72439302d.md). I believe these adjustments will help you find the information you need faster and with less frustration.

Lastly, we’ve synchronized our published strategy methodology documents and expanded their visibility within the documentation. This ensures that you have access to the most relevant strategies for your Fabric adoption journey, all in one place (Read more › /about/changes/2025-08-08-f359f7af9238c768ef15da3bd9b4a18af45e0d78.md). A heartfelt thank you to everyone in our community who contributed to these enhancements. Your input is invaluable, and I’m excited to see how these changes will positively impact your experience! ## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This month, we made a significant change by removing the "What's New" documentation section from our website. While it might seem a bit bittersweet, this decision was made to streamline our content and enhance your overall experience with the Fabric Adoption Framework. By simplifying our structure, we hope to make it easier for you to find the information you need without any distractions (Read more › /about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

Why does this matter? Well, we believe that focusing on the core content will allow us to provide you with a clearer and more cohesive understanding of our framework. We want you to feel confident navigating our resources, and this change is a step toward achieving that goal. Your feedback has been invaluable in guiding our decisions, and I want to extend a heartfelt thank you to everyone who contributed their thoughts and insights (Read more › /about/changes/2025-06-03-3a35144aa92198574f983f6a3054dd2b5db3ab9c.md).

We’re excited about the potential this change holds for our community and look forward to hearing how it impacts your journey with us. Thank you for being a part of this evolving story!
