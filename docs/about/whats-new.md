# What's new

## August 2025

Hello, dear readers! I’m excited to share some wonderful updates from August that make our Fabric Adoption Framework documentation even more user-friendly and accessible. We’ve made significant strides in enhancing the structure and clarity of our content. For instance, we’ve expanded the visibility of our strategy category, which means you'll now find it easier to navigate and locate the strategies that best suit your needs (Read more ›). Additionally, we’ve made some thoughtful adjustments to our sidebar categories, collapsing them for better clarity and a more streamlined experience (Read more ›).

We also added a new License section to our documentation, along with a direct link in the navigation menu. This ensures that you can easily find important licensing information, reinforcing our commitment to transparency and accessibility (Read more ›). These changes not only improve your experience but also reflect our ongoing dedication to fostering a supportive community around Fabric adoption.

I want to extend a heartfelt thank you to everyone who contributed to these updates. Your input and engagement are invaluable as we strive to create a welcoming and informative space for all users. I hope you enjoy exploring these enhancements as much as we enjoyed bringing them to life!
