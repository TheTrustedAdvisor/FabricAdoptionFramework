# What's new

## August 2025

Hello, dear readers! I’m excited to share some fresh updates from our Fabric Adoption Framework this August. We’ve been busy enhancing the documentation to make your experience smoother and more intuitive. One of the most significant changes is the addition of a new License section, which you can now find in our navigation menu. This is an important step in ensuring that you have clear access to the licensing information you need as you explore our resources (Read more ›).

Thank you to everyone who contributed to these changes! Your feedback and support are invaluable in helping us create a welcoming and user-friendly environment. I hope these updates enhance your journey with us as you adopt Fabric strategies. If you’re curious about all the details, feel free to check out the full list of changes this month (Read more ›). ## June 2025

Hello dear readers! This June, I want to share some significant changes we've made to the Fabric Adoption Framework documentation that I believe will enhance your experience. We’ve decided to remove the "What's New" documentation section entirely. This change was made with the intention of streamlining our content and making it easier for you to find the information you need without sifting through updates that may not be relevant to your journey. You can read more about this decision [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

I understand that this might feel like a big shift, but I truly believe it will lead to a more cohesive and user-friendly experience. This change reflects our commitment to listening to your feedback and continuously improving how we serve you. A heartfelt thank you to everyone who contributed to this discussion and helped shape this decision.

As we move forward, I’m excited to see how these adjustments will positively impact your navigation and overall experience with our documentation.
