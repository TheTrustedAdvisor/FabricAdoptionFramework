August has been a vibrant month for the Fabric Adoption Framework, and I'm excited to share some meaningful updates that enhance your experience with our documentation. I want to take a moment to thank our contributors and the community for their ongoing support and input, which continues to shape our documentation into a resource that truly serves your needs. ## June 2025

Hello, dear readers! This month, we've made a significant change to our documentation structure that I want to share with you. We’ve decided to remove the "What's New" section from our site. I know this may come as a surprise, but this change is aimed at streamlining our content and making it easier for you to find the information you need without sifting through updates that might not be relevant to your journey. You can read more about this decision [here](https://fabricadoptionframework.com/about/changes/2025-06-03-5a7d4f72ccbbd73c700b77c1b485216d1e29c0ea.md).

This shift matters because it reflects our commitment to enhancing your experience as you navigate our resources. By simplifying the structure, we hope to create a more intuitive environment that allows you to focus on what truly matters—adopting and implementing the Fabric framework in your projects. We believe this will lead to a more seamless experience and empower you to find the guidance you need more efficiently.

I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to our contributors and the community for their ongoing support and feedback. Your insights are invaluable in shaping our documentation to better serve you. As we move forward, I’m excited about the positive impact these changes will bring. Together, let’s continue to foster a space where everyone can thrive!
